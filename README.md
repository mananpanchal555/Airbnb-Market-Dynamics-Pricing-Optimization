# Airbnb Market Dynamics & Pricing Optimization

## Executive Summary
This project analyzes a dataset of 6,100+ active Airbnb listings to extract business intelligence regarding optimal pricing strategies, neighborhood revenue dynamics, and customer satisfaction metrics. The objective is to provide actionable recommendations for real estate investors and property managers to maximize occupancy rates and nightly revenue.

## Business Value & Strategic Outcomes
* **Pricing Optimization:** Identified price ceilings and median revenue expectations across "Entire Home," "Private Room," and "Shared Room" classifications.
* **Geographic Market Sizing:** Mapped the top 10 highest-density neighborhoods, cross-referencing average nightly prices to identify undervalued investment zones.
* **Customer Sentiment Analysis:** Correlated perceived "Value" scores with "Overall Rating" metrics to isolate the service factors that drive repeat bookings.

## Technical Stack
* **Business Intelligence:** Tableau (Interactive Data Visualization)
* **Data Pipeline:** Python 3.x (Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib)
* **Dataset Scope:** 6,173 properties, 22 variables (Pricing, Geo-coordinates, Host metrics, Review scoring).
